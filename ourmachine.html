STM 850 Vertical Machining Center

  • High-performance CNC machine used in die & mold making, aerospace, and automotive industries
  • Robust 'A' shaped column with multiple cross ribs offers high cutting performance and excellent stability
  • Equipped with high-precision **LM Roller Guideways** on all three axes for enhanced dynamics and longer machine life
  • Features an imported spindle from Taiwan with a G1 balanced grade for a superior surface finish
  • High efficiency ensured with rapid feed rates of up to 36 m/min
  • CNC Controller options available, including the advanced Mitsubishi M80
  • Table size of **1000 x 500 mm** with a maximum load capacity of up to **800 kg**

STM VL/VD 1050 Vertical Machining Center

  • Standard vertical machining center (VMC) for general purpose milling
  • Spindle speeds up to 8,000 RPM (belt type standard)
  • Equipped with a spacious table size of 1200 x 600 mm
  • Table load capacity typically up to 1,000 kgs
  • Suitable for various sectors including general engineering, automotive, and mold making
  • Manufacturer: S&T Machinery (STM)

BFW Omega 3050 High-Speed VMC

  • High-speed vertical machining center (VMC) for precision milling
  • Spindle speeds up to 24,000 RPM (optional), 12,000 RPM standard
  • Equipped with high rapid traverse rates of up to 60 m/min in X/Y axes and 48 m/min in Z-axis
  • Designed for high productivity with a fast tool change time of 1.4 seconds
  • Suitable for various sectors including electronics, automotive, and aerospace
  • Manufacturer: Bharat Fritz Werner (BFW)
