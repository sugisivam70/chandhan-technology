ABOUT  

At Chandhan Technology, we specialise in high-precision VMC drilling & milling — delivering highly accurate vertical machining solutions for aerospace, automotive, defence and industrial customers. Our focus is on process stability, tight tolerances, rapid turnaround and parts that meet exacting inspection standards.

From precision drilling cycles and multi-axis milling to fixture design, probing, and in-process inspection, our VMC workflows are engineered for repeatability and low scrap rates. We combine hardened tooling, optimised feeds & speeds, and shop-floor automation to deliver consistent results at scale.

Why clients choose Chandhan Technology

VMC Drilling & Milling Advantages

With 9+ years of specialised experience in vertical machining, we provide CNC program optimisation, high-speed spindle capability, automatic tool changers (ATC), and tight 3D inspection controls — enabling complex parts with tolerances down to ±0.005 mm.
